About US


Welcome to Cricket Uncovered, your premier destination for cricket analysis.

Our mission is to provide cricket enthusiasts, analysts, and professionals with the most accurate and insightful data analysis of cricket performances. Our platform is designed to analyze cricket data meticulously, focusing on identifying the best players in key roles such as openers, middle-order batsmen, all-rounders, bowlers, and the top 11 players of the tournament. We use advanced data analytics techniques to break down player performances and present the findings in an easy-to-understand and visually appealing format. At Cricket Uncovered, we believe that data can tell powerful stories. Our team comprises data scientists, cricket analysts, and technology experts dedicated to bringing you the best cricket analysis possible. Our visualizations, powered by Power BI, allow you to explore the data interactively and gain deeper insights into the game. Whether you're a fan looking to understand the game better, a coach aiming to improve team performance, or a player seeking to refine your skills, Cricket Uncovered is here to provide the insights you need. Join us in our quest to uncover the hidden gems of cricket through data analysis. Together, we can enhance our understanding of this beautiful game.
